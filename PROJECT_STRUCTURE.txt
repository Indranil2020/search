RELIABILITY COLOR CODING
========================

GREEN  (score 0.8-1.0) - High reliability
  - Peer-reviewed in reputable journals
  - High citation count (100+)
  - Verified across multiple databases
  - From known publishers

YELLOW (score 0.5-0.79) - Moderate reliability
  - Preprints not yet peer-reviewed
  - Newer papers with few citations
  - Single source verification
  - Conference papers

RED    (score 0.0-0.49) - Lower reliability
  - Unverified sources
  - Retracted papers
  - Contradicted findings
  - Grey literature without peer review
